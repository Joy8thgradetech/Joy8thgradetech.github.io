What Is WhiteBox Learning?

WhiteBox Learning is a complete Standards-Based STEM Learning System for Engineering, Science, and Technology. Using this platform I can create, analyze, and simulate designs, hundreds of times, from a web browser before creating a physical model. Through out journey I can also compete with other students as well!

What Is SketchUp?

SketchUp is an intuitive 3D modeling application that lets you create and edit 2D and 3D models. The program includes drawing layout functionality and surface rendering. The app has a wide range of applications, including in the worlds of architecture, interior design, landscaping, and video game design. Sketchup has also found success with people who want to create, share, or download 3D models for use with 3D printers.

What Is Vex Robotics?

VEX Robotics is a robitics kit that promotes creativity, problem solving and teamwork through the engineering of a robot. The kit provides students with all the materials, maual instuctions, and a coded remote controller. In addition to learning valuable engineering skills, students gain life skills such as teamwork, perseverance, communication, collaboration, project management, and critical thinking.
